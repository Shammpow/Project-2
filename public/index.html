Health Warnings

Recipes posted on this site can come from anyone and anywhere. Some may be intended to be taken seriously, others...not so much.

Use good judgement when browsing our site and, as always, never consume anything harmful to your health:

  • Nothing toxic
  • Nothing you're allergic to
  • No overindulging in alcohol
  • No underage drinking
  • No drunk driving
  • No drunk spellcasting
  • No consumption of magical substances without a high-level wizard present

Oh, and no wasting years of your life searching for fictional and/or supernatural ingredients...that's not a health warning per se, just good advice.

Stay safe. Stay healthy. Stay nerdy. Have fun.
